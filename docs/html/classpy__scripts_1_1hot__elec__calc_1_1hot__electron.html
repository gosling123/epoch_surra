def py_scripts.hot_elec_calc.hot_electron.get_energy_frac (   self)

get_energy_frac

Estimates the fraction of outgoing energy being due to hot electrons. Taken to be the ratio of the area under the curve of the hot electron region to the area of the whole distribution. The hot electron region is assumed to be zero if distribution never signifcantly deviates from the initial Maxwellian.

Parameters
selfThe object pointer
nNumber of segments (default is 3)
plot(Logical) Calculate average value across various number of fits
plot(Logical) Plot T_hot vs number of fits
